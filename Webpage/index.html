We Scale With You

HireUs2toHire is a talent consultancy here to help you and your recruitment needs.
HireUs2toHire was founded to revitalise the technology and digital recruitment industry. We are a talent consultancy that is expert in advising small to medium scale-ups with their recruitment process in the digital and tech space. Our founder, Jimmy Grice, is a passionate and devoted talent consultant with over 10 years' experience in the Australian and New Zealand markets. He has an ambition to help scale out product, design, technology, sales and infrastructure teams, providing a premium and transparent experience end to end.

Our values

Commitment

We are dedicated to you and your recruitment needs, whether thats as an employer or a job seeker. We will work to the best of our ability to carefully match the right candidates to the best role.

Transparency

Transparency is key. We will ensure we keep you informed throughout the entire recruitment process. We will also give you access to some of our tools we use for searching, so you feel like you are on the journey with us.

Partnership

Our goal is to build long serving relationships with both our clients and candidates. The way we work with you is highly collaborative, as this builds trust and makes for a positive long-term partnership with our network.
